About



Tino Selić is a Helsinki-based digital artist, designer, and creative developer.



His work spans a wide range of new media—3D modeling and animation, digital painting and illustration, development of games, websites and other interactive audiovisual experiences, as well as reviving vintage technology through creative projects.


Art


Tino's art is characterized by atmospheric and thought-provoking images that blend realism and surrealism through immersive experiences. He explores themes of nature, technology, and human emotions, weaving together narratives of fiction and reality, often stemming from personal experiences and cultural exchange. Frequently inspired by Impressionism, color and light play a crucial role in his work, too. With meticulous attention to detail and a spirit of experimentation, Tino pushes creative limits.


Design


As a designer, Tino values ​​precision and clarity, putting design theory into practice via design principles, UX, and user goals. His design process prioritizes empathy and inclusion, ensuring that every decision considers the needs and problems of the end users.
