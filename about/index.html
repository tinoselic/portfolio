About



Hi, my name is Tino Selić (he/him). I am a versatile digital artist and designer whose work spans a wide range of media—from 3D modelling and animation to websites, games, and interactive experiences.



I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Interactive Media from Augsburg University of Applied Sciences in 2021. Currently, I am studying New Media (Master of Arts) with a minor in Game Design and Development at Aalto University in Espoo, Finland.

In spring 2019, I participated in the Erasmus+ programme and spent a semester abroad at the University of Lapland in Rovaniemi, Finland. During this time, I experienced the beauty of Finland's natural phenomena—the northern lights, the polar night, and the midnight sun. Since then, these extraordinary scenarios have been a great source of inspiration for my artistic work. Conceptually, I like to explore themes from nature, technology, and human emotions, often weaving narratives from fiction, personal experiences, and cultural exchange.

I speak German, English, and a bit of Finnish.




Contact me: tino.selic@gmail.com
