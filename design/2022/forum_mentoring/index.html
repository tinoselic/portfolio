Quality Standards Brochure

Layout and graphic design of a brochure for updated quality standards



Following the client's briefing, I applied the existing corporate design to a new brochure design. For this, I chose a square format of 210 x 210 mm, as it provides enough space for clarity and legibility of the text while remaining compact and handy. The main colour of each chapter changes alternately between red and blue, making it easier for the reader to distinguish and find the chapters. Title pages for each chapter, with illustrated graphics representing the chapter's content, further aid clarity and enhance the brochure's appeal. An important part of the brochure is the interactive checklist, which includes an instruction page on how the list and checkboxes work as well as columns of lines to give the reader the possibility to write clean marginal notes.
