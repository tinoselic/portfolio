About


I was born and raised in Augsburg, Germany, where I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Interactive Media from Augsburg University of Applied Sciences in 2021. Currently, I am studying New Media (Master of Arts) at Aalto University in Espoo, Finland.

In spring 2019, I spent a semester abroad at the University of Lapland in Rovaniemi, Finland, where I delved into Finland’s natural phenomena such as the northern lights, the midnight sun, and the polar night. Since then, these extraordinary lighting situations have been a great source of inspiration for my work.

I am passionate about visual and immersive art that converges technology and creativity. My favorite works include light art installations, 3D art, animation, and games. I mainly work with Unreal Engine, Blender, and the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, After Effects).
