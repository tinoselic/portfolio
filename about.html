About


Hallo. My name is Tino Selic, I am a Helsinki-based digital artist and designer.

I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Interactive Media from Augsburg University of Applied Sciences in 2021. Currently, I am studying New Media (Master of Arts) at Aalto University in Espoo, Finland.

I am passionate about animation and 3D, as well as interactive experiences, websites, and games. My favourite tools are Unreal Engine, Blender, TouchDesigner, and the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, and After Effects). I am also familiar with programming in Java, Javascript, HTML, and CSS. I speak German, English, and a bit of Finnish.
