Piru

3D Game Art
2023

Psychological horror retro-fps game about a demonic ritual at a cabin in the wintry forests of Lapland

Download and play for free: viitahenri.itch.io/piru

I participated in the game project seminar in autumn 2023 at Aalto University as part of my game design minor. As a 3D artist, I created some of the assets for Piru by modelling in Blender and texturing in Photoshop. I focused on keeping the polycounts of most objects low (10-500 triangles) and texture resolutions at 128x128 pixels to achieve the desired low-res PS1 game look. Additionally, we used a special retro shader for the URP in Unity. I created some of the textures by taking pictures with my smartphone camera, such as my teammate's hand and the trees.

Shader: github.com/Kodrin/URP-PSX
Software: Blender (modelling), Photoshop (texturing), Unity



Asset Collection

Trees


The trees consist of a hexagonal cylinder for the tree trunk and three planes aligned at a 60° angle for the leaves. I added snow to textures with the colour curve in Photoshop. In Unity, I then set up the textures as alpha-textures, and assigned them as the base-colours for the leaves' two-sided materials.
