Space Jellyfish

I started doing 3D in 2018 during the 2nd year of my bachelor's studies in Interactive Media. In a game design class, I created a short loop animation of a jellyfish floating through space that I used as a teaser and start screen for the game prototype. I mainly followed this tutorial and made changes in texture, light, and colours.
