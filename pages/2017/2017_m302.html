M 3.02

Abstract Painting
2017

A series of paintings in the style of Gerhard Richter

During an etching course taught by Anda Manea I took in my Bachelor's studies, I did a creative exercise to better understand the colours and composition of abstract paintings. Just like famous painter Gerhard Richter, I worked only with acrylic paint in primary colours, paintbrushes and the doctor blade to create a series of four abstract paintings.
