About



Hi, my name is Tino Selić (he/him). I am a versatile digital artist and designer whose work spans a wide range of media—from 3D modelling and animation to websites, games, and interactive experiences.



I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Interactive Media from Augsburg University of Applied Sciences in 2021. Currently, I am studying New Media (Master of Arts) at Aalto University in Espoo, Finland.

In spring 2019, I participated in the Erasmus+ programme and spent a semester abroad at the University of Lapland in Rovaniemi, Finland. During this time, I experienced the beauty of Finland's natural phenomena—the northern lights, the polar night, and the midnight sun. Since then, these extraordinary scenarios have been a wonderful source of inspiration for colour and light in my artistic work. Conceptually, I like to explore themes of nature, technology, and human emotion, often weaving narratives about the past and the future.

My favourite tools are Unreal Engine, Blender, TouchDesigner, and the Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, and After Effects). I am also familiar with programming in Java (Processing), C++ (Unreal Engine), JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

I speak German, English, and a bit of Finnish.
