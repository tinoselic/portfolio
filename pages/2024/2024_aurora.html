AURORA

Master's Thesis: Interactive Experience
2024

Children Expressing Friendship Through an Interactive Experience

AURORA is an interactive experience that uses the ZED 2i stereo camera and Unreal Engine 5 to create virtual auroras that follow users' movements. On 16 April 2024, a total of 21 third-graders from the Helsinki German School participated in a project during afternoon care. The children were divided into four groups and asked to interact with AURORA to express friendship.

Software:
ZED SDK, Unreal Engine 5

Setup


My assistant Raphael and I set up the project at the gym hall of the Helsinki German School.



Friendship Bracelets


As a reward for their participation, I made friendship bracelets of cotton yarn for the children.
